Spaghetti

Classic spaghetti with marinara sauce, a quick and delicious dinner option!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound spaghetti pasta
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Fresh basil (optional)
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil water in a large pot, add salt, and cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a separate pan, heat olive oil and warm marinara sauce.
  3. Once spaghetti is cooked, drain and mix with the marinara sauce.
  4. Top with fresh basil and Parmesan cheese if desired.
