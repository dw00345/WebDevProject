Oven Filet Mignon

A tender and juicy filet mignon, perfectly roasted in the oven for a flavorful and elegant dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 2 filet mignon steaks
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season the filet mignon with salt, pepper, and thyme.
  3. Heat olive oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat and sear the steaks for 2-3 minutes per side.
  4. Transfer the skillet to the oven and roast for 6-8 minutes for medium-rare, or longer for desired doneness.
