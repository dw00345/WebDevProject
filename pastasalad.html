Pasta Salad

This refreshing pasta salad is perfect for lunch or a light meal!

Ingredients:

  • Pasta (bowtie, penne, or rotini)
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Olives
  • Italian dressing

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions, then drain and cool.
  2. Chop the vegetables and add them to the pasta.
  3. Drizzle with Italian dressing and toss to combine.
