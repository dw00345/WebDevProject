Pizza Bagels

Quick and easy pizza bagels, perfect for a fun snack or lunch!

Ingredients:

  • 4 bagel halves
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Optional: pepperoni or vegetables

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Spread marinara sauce on the bagel halves.
  3. Top with shredded mozzarella and your choice of toppings.
  4. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  5. Enjoy your delicious pizza bagels!
