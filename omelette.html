Omelette

Perfectly fluffy omelette filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, and meat. Great for breakfast or brunch!

Ingredients: Eggs, milk, cheese, spinach, mushrooms, onions.

Instructions: Whisk eggs and pour into a hot pan. Add fillings and cook until set. Fold and serve.
