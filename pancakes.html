Pancakes

Fluffy, golden pancakes perfect for a delicious breakfast. Serve with syrup, fruit, or whipped cream for extra flavor!

Ingredients: Flour, eggs, milk, sugar, baking powder, butter.

Instructions: Mix all ingredients together and cook on a hot griddle. Flip when bubbles form.
