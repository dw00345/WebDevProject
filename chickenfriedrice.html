Chicken Fried Rice

A flavorful chicken fried rice perfect for dinner, loaded with veggies and protein!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice (preferably cold)
  • 1 chicken breast, diced
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 2 eggs
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Green onions (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the chicken breast in a skillet with a bit of sesame oil until browned.
  2. Push the chicken to one side and scramble eggs on the other side of the skillet.
  3. Add the rice, veggies, and soy sauce to the skillet and stir-fry until everything is heated through.
  4. Top with chopped green onions and serve.
