Caesar Salad

This classic Caesar salad is a healthy and delicious lunch option!

Ingredients:

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Caesar dressing
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Chop the lettuce and place it in a large bowl.
  2. Drizzle with Caesar dressing and toss to coat.
  3. Top with croutons and freshly grated Parmesan cheese.
