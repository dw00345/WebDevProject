Gourmet Bacon Burger

This gourmet bacon burger is stacked with delicious layers of flavors!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 4 slices of crispy bacon
  • 4 burger buns
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce, grilled onions, pickles
  • Ketchup, mustard

Instructions:

  1. Form ground beef into 4 patties and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the patties on a grill or in a skillet until they reach desired doneness.
  3. Toast the burger buns on the grill.
  4. Assemble the burgers with cheese, bacon, lettuce, tomato, pickles, and condiments of choice.
