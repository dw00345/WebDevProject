Crispy Potato Chips

Homemade crispy potato chips that are a perfect snack for any occasion!

Ingredients:

  • 4 medium potatoes, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: paprika or garlic powder for extra flavor

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Thinly slice the potatoes using a mandoline or sharp knife.
  3. Toss the potato slices in olive oil and season with salt.
  4. Spread the potato slices in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 15-20 minutes, flipping halfway through, until golden and crispy.
  6. Allow to cool and enjoy!
