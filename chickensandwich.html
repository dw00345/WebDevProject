Chicken Sandwich

A tasty and satisfying chicken sandwich for a perfect lunch!

Ingredients:

  • Grilled chicken breast
  • Sandwich bread or bun
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Mayonnaise

Instructions:

  1. Grill the chicken breast until fully cooked.
  2. Toast the bread or bun and spread mayonnaise on one side.
  3. Place the grilled chicken, lettuce, and tomato on the bread.
