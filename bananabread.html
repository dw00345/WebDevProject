Banana Bread

Soft, moist banana bread that's perfect for breakfast or a snack.

Ingredients:

  • 2 to 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, combine mashed bananas, melted butter, and sugar.
  3. Stir in the eggs and milk until well combined.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, mixing until just combined.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased loaf pan and bake for 60-65 minutes.
  7. Let cool before slicing and serving.
