Grilled Cheese

This classic grilled cheese sandwich is quick, easy, and perfect for any lunch!

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1-2 slices of cheese
  • Butter

Instructions:

  1. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  2. Place cheese between the slices, with the buttered sides facing out.
  3. Grill the sandwich in a skillet until golden brown on both sides.
